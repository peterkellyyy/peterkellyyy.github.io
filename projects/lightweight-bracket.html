FEA

Lightweight Mounting Bracket

An iterative bracket redesign balancing stiffness, mass, machining constraints, and factor-of-safety targets.

Overview

Reworked a mounting bracket to remove unnecessary material while preserving stiffness around fastener interfaces.

Engineering Focus

  • Defined boundary conditions and compared first-pass FEA results across design variants.
  • Maintained practical cutter access, fillet sizes, and flat datum surfaces.
  • Documented design tradeoffs for review with manufacturing stakeholders.

Outcome

The final concept reduced mass while keeping stress concentrations away from critical attachment points.

Project Details

Tools
CAD, FEA, design review
Role
Analysis-driven redesign
