Compact Pump Skid Layout

A packaged fluid system layout organized around service access, compact routing, and vibration isolation.

Overview

Created a layout study for a compact pump skid, organizing equipment, frame members, pipe routing, and maintenance clearances.

Engineering Focus

  • Checked service envelope around pump, motor, valves, and instrumentation.
  • Routed connections to minimize sharp bends and unsupported spans.
  • Planned frame attachment points and vibration isolation features.

Outcome

The layout clarified packaging constraints early and gave stakeholders a clean basis for detailed component selection.

Project Details

Tools
CAD layout, packaging review, BOM planning
Role
System layout design
Deliverables
Layout model, clearance notes, routing study
