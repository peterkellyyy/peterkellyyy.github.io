Robotic Gripper Prototype

A compact end-effector concept for compliant handling of small parts in an automated workflow.

Overview

Built a gripper concept around compact actuation, replaceable fingers, and predictable compliance for varied part geometries.

Engineering Focus

  • Compared jaw profiles for part capture and release reliability.
  • Kept the actuator package compact for wrist clearance.
  • Designed modular fingers to simplify iteration during testing.

Outcome

The prototype architecture supported quick finger swaps and repeatable pickup trials without redesigning the full end effector.

Project Details

Tools
CAD, prototype fabrication, fit checks
Role
Mechanical concept design
Deliverables
Prototype model, test notes, iteration plan
