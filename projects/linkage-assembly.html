Mechanism

Adjustable Linkage Assembly

Motion-control linkage designed for compact packaging, predictable travel, and easy adjustment during assembly.

Overview

Created a linkage concept for controlled angular motion in a tight envelope, with adjustment features for prototype tuning.

Engineering Focus

  • Modeled travel limits and checked interference across the full range of motion.
  • Defined pivot hardware, bearing surfaces, and clearance assumptions.
  • Reviewed tolerance stack-up for repeatable end positions.

Outcome

The design supported simple fabrication while giving the team a clear path for motion validation and future refinement.
